Personal Safety

  • Do not send money to someone you haven't met in person.
  • When meeting someone for first time, please remember to meet on public and open places like cafe, bank, shopping center, etc.
  • Do not meet with a seller/ buyer in a secluded / hidden place, do not invite strangers at home.
  • Consider having a friend accompany you when meeting with buyer or seller.
  • Never give out financial info (Bank account, social security, etc.) to anyone when buying online.
